My Vision

My vision is to build a robust space program that will not only propel Morocco to the forefront of space exploration but also foster technological innovation and sustainability across Africa. Through public-private partnerships, strategic collaborations, and a strong commitment to anti-corruption, we will create a legacy of progress.
