MASA: Morocco's Space Program

The Moroccan Agency for Space and Advanced Technologies (MASA) will be the cornerstone of Morocco's space ambitions. With the establishment of a launch pad in Dakhla, we aim to become leaders in satellite launches and space-based services, fostering international collaborations and driving economic growth.
